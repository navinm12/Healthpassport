About us

Healthpassport makes it easier for patients to grant permission for their medical data to be used for the clinical trials. It uses blockchain technology to securely store health records and maintain a single version of the truth. Individuals will be for handling their own records thus, to be secure from breaches and modification.

Our services

Blockchain

Implementing Blockchain technology allows patients to assign access rules for their medical data, With this technology, patients can connect to other hospitals and collect their medical data

Consent

In healthcare consent allows you to participate in your own medical care. Also, informed consent allows you to make decisions with your healthcare provider

Verification

Two step verification is a feature that adds more security to the patient records. this verification process enable to verify the patient and accompanied by six digit pin.

ChatBot Assistant

Chatbot uses artificial intelligence techniques along with machine learning technologies. This enable users to enter symptoms to predict the disease.
